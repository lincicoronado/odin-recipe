Spaghetti alla Carbonara!

Quick and easy Authentic carbonara recipe from Italy.

Click me to watch the video of the recipe.

Ingredients for 2 portions

  • 100gr of Guanciale or Pancetta
  • 3 Eggs
  • 80gr Pecorino Romano (cheese)
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 200gr Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Start by stir frying 50 gr of guanciale per person (no oil!).
  2. As the guanciale is sizzling, boil the pasta (donâ€™t forget to save about 1 cup of pasta water!).
  3. Add 1 egg yolk for person and one extra for the pot.
  4. Grate about 40 gr of pecorino cheese per person.
  5. Mix the pecorino into the egg yolk to make a smooth cream.
  6. Add freshly ground peppercorn
  7. Then add a little bit of fried guanciale and fat into the egg mixture.
  8. Toss the pasta and cream then mix with passion!

    9. Useful tip:

    Be gorgeous while cooking. It makes it taste better.
