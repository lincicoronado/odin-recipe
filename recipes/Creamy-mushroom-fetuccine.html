Creamy Mushroom Fetuccine

Traditional Fettuccine Panna & Funghi (cream and mushrooms)

A really creamy pasta that combines mushrooms with garlic, butter and a lot of parmesan cheese.

Click me to watch the video of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 50gr Salted Butter
  • 250gr Mushroom
  • 200gr Parmigiano (parmesan cheese)
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 2 Glasses of white wine
  • A bunch of parsley
  • Salt as needed

    • Steps

    1. Melt the butter and ass chopped garlic to it. Stir fry for a few minutes and add chopped mushrooms with the parsley.
    2. Cook until the mushrooms are soft then add two glasses of white wine.
    3. Put your flame on high to burn the alcohol off for a few minutes.
    4. Turn your flame back down and add 1 pint of heavy cream and half of your Parmesan, stir and add salt as needed.
    5. Cook your pasta in a hot water pan and salt it.
    6. Once pasta is rinsed add to the pan with the cream sauce in it and stir.
    7. Serve with some parsley and Parmesan garnish
